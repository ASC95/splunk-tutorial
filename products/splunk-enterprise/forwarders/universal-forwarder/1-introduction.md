# System requirements
- Recommended: Dual-core 1.5GHz+ processor, 1GB+ RAM
- Minimum: 1.0Ghz processor, 512MB RAM, 5GB of free disk space 
# Terminology
- The Splunk instance that receives data from a Splunk forwarder is referred to as a "receiver"
